About Us

about us

Foodie was founded in 2017 with the mission to provide delicious recipes without compromising nutritional value. Since its beginning, its team has been passionate about finding the most nutritionally complementary vegetables, fruits, meats, grains, and more.

Foodie takes the guesswork out of the equation with sophisticated cutting-edge lab testing and experimentation. Various recipes can be paired together to create a truly remarkable meal -not only for your tastebuds, but for your entire health and well-being.

This year, Foodie is taking cooking to the next level by providing recipes that are vegan and vegetarian. Experience the joy of 100% health with 100% of the nutrients your body craves.
