Recipe

Squash Soup

Set your oven to 450F. Cut the squash in half and place both halves on a baking sheet. Place the squash in the oven. Meanwhile, heat 2TB ghee in a heavy pan on the stove. Add 1/2 tsp of each of the spices and the onion. Let it sizzle for a few minutes. When the onions are browned, add the chopped tomatoes and carrots. Add just enough water or broth to cover this mixture. Cover and simmer for 10 minutes. Take the squash out of the oven when it is soft. Add it to the rest of the pan. Let simmer for a few more minutes until the flavors have mixed. Finally remove from heat and run the mixture through a blender or food processor. Garnish with apple shavings and raisins.

Ingredients

  • 1 spaghetti squash
  • spices (cinnamon, allspice, nutmeg, cumin, bay leaf)
  • ghee
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 3 carrots
  • 2 apples
  • 100g raisins
